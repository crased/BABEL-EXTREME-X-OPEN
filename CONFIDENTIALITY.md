# Confidentiality

The contents of this repository — including the brief, sample inputs, schema,
rubric, and any private materials shared with you — are confidential to
LunarTech LABS.

## What this means in practice

- **Do not host this assignment on a public GitHub repository, GitLab,
  Bitbucket, blog, gist, pastebin, Notion page, or any other public surface.**
- Do not post screenshots, sample inputs, or your solution to social media,
  Discord, Slack, Reddit, Stack Overflow, or other public forums.
- Do not share the assignment or your solution with anyone who is not part
  of your own evaluation process.
- Do not feed the assignment text or the sample inputs into a third-party
  AI service whose terms permit them to train on or retain your inputs.
  (Most consumer chat tools do this. Most paid API tiers do not. Read the
  terms before pasting anything in.)

## Submission

- Fork this repo to your own **private** account.
- Push your work to your private fork.
- Share access via private invitation to the email we used to send you the
  assignment.
- Tag the commit you want us to evaluate.

## After evaluation

- We will not publish your submission.
- You retain copyright over the code you wrote.
- You may discuss the *general shape* of the problem you worked on (e.g.
  "I built a scanned-document extraction pipeline for an apprentice
  evaluation"). You may not publish specifics, screenshots, or the bundled
  inputs.
- If you'd like to use any code you wrote here in a public portfolio later,
  ask us first.

## If something goes wrong

If you accidentally pushed to a public fork, or shared the assignment with
the wrong person, or pasted something into a logging service: tell us
immediately. We grade honesty highly.
